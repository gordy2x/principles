mesocosms.csv contains three variables

Mesocosm :  mesocosm idenifier
swamp : swamp identifier 
veg - vegetation type, tt - Ti-tree thicket, ch - Cyperoid heath

wetland_biomass.csv contains 8 variables

Mesocosm :  mesocosm idenifier
swamp : swamp identifier 
days : days since treatment began
veg - vegetation type, tt - Ti-tree thicket, ch - Cyperoid heath
water - water treatment high (H), medium (M) or low (L)
biomass - above ground biomass in grams
